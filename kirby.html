Weekend Camping Kirby Cove - Marin HeadLands - 5/10/2020 - G3

Where did you go: Biked to Kirby Cove from market st

When: 7/7/2020

What were the conditions: 70*F when we left, and a full moon came up!

Who did you go with: Eoin, Colin, Kate

What happened: we all set off from the Ferry building, biked over the bridge and up the hill down to the cove. we brought stoves and had hot chocolate and whiskey, and slept on the beech. a racoon stole our food in the night, in the morning we rode back to the city, and went to work. 

What went well: I love whiskey

What could have improved: should have stashed out food better

What will you do next time: use the lockers
