Weekend Camping Kirby Cove - Marin HeadLands - 5/10/2020 - G3

Where did you go: Mt Whitney Summit via the Mountaineers route.

When: 07/04/2020

What were the conditions: Overcast and cloudy on the first day, no visible snow, sunny on the 2nd day. 

Who did you go with: Jeff

What happened: Hiked from whitney portal the 1st day up to boy scout lakes, then climbed up the shoot to the summit, left at 2am for a 8am summit. We stayed up for 20 min, then returned to camp the same way we came up, packed up, and hiked to the car (finished by 5pm)

Gear required: Typical backpacking gear, it was above 40 the whole time.

What went well: Gear was great, wonderful trip overall

What could have improved: got a little lost in the ledges

What will you do next time: check the gps in the ledges area
